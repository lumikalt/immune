#+title: HSC

* Hematopoietic Stem Cells
HSC in the red bone marrow differentiate into red blood cells, white blood cells and platelets.

* Differentiation Lineages
HSC differentiates into Myeloid stem cells or Lymphoid stem cells

** Myeloid Stem Cells
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       b17487fa-63a0-48d1-b027-4c6de071d60e
:END:
The Myeloid Lineage.
These further differentiate into:
- Megakaryoblasts
  - Megakaryocytes
    - Platelets
- Proerythroblasts
  - Reticulocytes
    - Erythocytes
- Mast Cells[fn:1]
- Myeloblasts
  - Basophils[fn:1]
  - Neutrophils[fn:1]
  - Eosinophils[fn:1]
- Monoblasts
  - [[id:d0543e0a-ef0b-4990-b67b-c68084aa2467][Monocytes]]
    - [[id:96a95dcb-e977-4788-8c70-8e110e6cde04][Macrophages]]
    - [[id:0c0acdcc-f093-41d2-a7b8-0db4a99478ad][Dentritic Cells]]

** Lymphoid Stem Cells
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       9388b024-7c46-49f2-aa1c-af5b929080e4
:END:
The Lymphoid Lineage.
These further differentiate into:
- Lymphoblasts
  - Natural Killer Cells
    (Large Granular Lymphocytes)
  - Small Lymphocytes
    - T Lymphocytes
    - B Lymphocytes
      - Plasma Cells
