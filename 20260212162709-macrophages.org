#+title: Macrophages
#+filetags: :cell:innate:

* Functions
- [[id:50e80900-a8db-4435-b457-12ea355843d9][Phagocytosis]] of pathogens and cellular debris, by consumption of dead and dying host cells.


* Types
** Tissue-Resident Macrophages
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       5f8008f2-7e30-45ad-b07c-b3008d091c05
:END:
These cells originate from the fetal yolk sack, rather than the bone marrow.
Almost every tissue has a specific type of these.
This kind of macrophage performs important roles during [[id:6821acc5-a87e-428f-b618-c02ec1942b0f][homeostasis]].
