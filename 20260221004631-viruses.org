#+title: Viruses
#+filetags: :pathogen:

[[id:0ba77d1b-d79e-4bc5-ad25-4b7163b7466c][Pathogen]] composed of a nucleic acid genome enclosed in a protein coat.
Viruses can replicate only in living cells, as they do not possess the metabolic machinery for independent life.
