#+title: Bacteria

A vast kingdom of unicellular prokaryotic microorganisms.
Some species cause infections in humans and animals, while others make up most of the body's [[id:20458481-b3b6-42c7-9a67-cd27629c9586][commensal microbiota]]. 
Disease-causing bacteria may live in extracellular spaces or inside cells within vesicles or the cytosol.
