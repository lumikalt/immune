#+title: Specificity

Probability of a negative test for a healthy patient. Cf. [[id:534f3093-d793-4240-9dbb-d7fe096e3dd1][sensitivity]].

\begin{align*}
  specificity &= \frac{\#TN}{\#TN + \#FP} \\
              &= \frac{\#TN}{\#\text{healthy individuals}}
\end{align*}

Wherein:
- True Negative: Healthy individual correctly identified as healthy;
- False Positive: Healthy individual incorrectly identified as unhealthy.
