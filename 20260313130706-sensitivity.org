#+title: Sensitivity

Probability of a positive test for an  unhealthy patient. Cf. [[id:55b092e6-9eb3-414b-aabd-62306b2b4d7f][specificity]].

\begin{align*}
  sensitivity &= \frac{\#TP}{\#TP + \#FN} \\
              &= \frac{\#TP}{\#\text{unhealthy individuals}}
\end{align*}

Wherein:
- True Positive: Unhealthy individual correctly identified as unhealthy;
- False Negative: Unhealthy individual incorrectly identified as healthy.
